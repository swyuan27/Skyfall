How to play (but no one actually reads these right)

This is an endless runner game. The goal is to get the highest possible score by staying alive as long as possible. Do this by avoiding the blocks, which are obstacles. For convenience, you can use the space key to restart the game.

Buffs: There are 3 primary boosts that come in the form of rings

Orange Ring: Time slow

This slows down everything. Your score will still increase at the same rate!

Green Ring: Shrink effect

Smaller is better. Not what she said, but it helps in this game

Pink Ring: Collision detection and Supervision

Objects you are about to hit straight on are highlighted. In addition, you can see better through the fog
